In addition, I have a close research collaboration with A/Prof. [Guodong Long](https://guodonglong.github.io/) and Dr. [Tao Shen](https://taoshen58.github.io/) from the University of Technology Sydney. In the past, I received a master‚Äôs degree in computer science at Fudan University, supervised by Prof. [Wenqiang Zhang](http://www.fudanroilab.com/2021/07/01/WenqiangZhang.html).

My research interests lie in **Large Language Model**, **Vision-and-Language**, and **Information Retrieval**.
