I am a final-year PhD student in computer science at the State Key Laboratory of Internet of Things for Smart City (SKL-IOTSC), University of Macau, under the supervision of Prof. [Jianbing Shen (IEEE Fellow)](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=_Q3NTToAAAAJ&hl=en). I completed my master's degree from Fudan University, supervised by Prof. [Wenqiang Zhang](http://www.fudanroilab.com/2021/07/01/WenqiangZhang.html). Throughout my academic journey, I have been fortunate to collaborate with Prof. [Yu Cheng](https://ych133.github.io/) from the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Dr. [Xiubo Geng](https://xiubo0211.github.io/) from Microsoft, Prof. [Guodong Long](https://guodonglong.github.io/) from the UTS, and Dr. [Tao Shen](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=SegyX9AAAAAJ&hl=en) from Oracle.

My research is centered on **Large Language Models**, spanning **architecture, reasoning, multimodal learning, and healthcare applications**. My research is supported by the Fundamental Research Project for Young Ph.D. students from NSFC (国家自然科学基金青年学生基础研究项目(博士生)). I was also selected for Tencent's "Project Up" Talent Program (青云计划). I have published over 30 CCF-A papers. In addition, I contribute to the community by serving as an Area Chair for top-tier AI conferences such as ACL, NeurIPS, KDD, and EMNLP. 

I am always open for research discussions and collaborations. If you are interested in discussing or collaborating, please feel free to contact me via [email](mailto:yucheng.zhou.cs@gmail.com).
